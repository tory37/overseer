Architectural Approaches

How should the "Overseer" feel and behave?

A

The Command Center

Layout: Flexible Grid.

Dashboard feel. Drag and drop terminals and tools into a custom grid. Great for multi-monitor or ultra-wide setups.

B

The IDE-like Workspace (Recommended)

Layout: Sidebar + Split-Pane Tabs.

Familiar and focused. Sidebar for repo organization; Center area for agent "tabs" with a toggleable secondary view (Shell/Git).

C

The Process Orchestrator

Layout: List/Tree Focused.

Optimized for volume. A dense tree of all active agents and their status. One-click to "maximize" a specific instance's terminal.
